About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you battle against endless waves of monsters in a chaotic world. Use your skills and strategic thinking to stay alive as long as possible while defeating creatures of increasing power and difficulty.

How to Play

Navigate your character using WASD or arrow keys, and automatically attack nearby monsters. Collect experience points to level up and choose new abilities. Survive as long as possible against increasingly difficult waves of enemies.

Endless Gameplay

Test your survival skills in an endless mode where monsters become progressively stronger and more numerous.

Character Progression

Level up your character and unlock powerful abilities to customize your playstyle and combat strategy.

Diverse Enemies

Face a wide variety of monsters, each with unique behaviors and attack patterns that will challenge your adaptability.
